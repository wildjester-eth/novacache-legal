Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 1, 2025  |  Effective: January 1, 2025

This policy applies to all NovaCache products and services sold through Etsy, Gumroad, and other platforms. It covers data collected directly by NovaCache. Each marketplace also has its own privacy policy which governs data they collect independently.

1. Who We Are

NovaCache ("we," "us," or "our") is an independent creative shop selling digital art prints, printable downloads, and print-on-demand physical products. We operate primarily through third-party marketplaces including Etsy and Gumroad.

Contact: christopher.d.raymond@gmail.com

2. Information We Collect

Information you provide directly

  • Order information: Your name, email address, and shipping address (for physical products) as provided during checkout on Etsy or Gumroad.
  • Communications: Messages you send us through marketplace messaging systems or by email.

Information collected automatically

  • Purchase data: Product purchased, price paid, date of transaction — provided to us by the marketplace.
  • Shop analytics: Aggregate view counts and click data provided by marketplace dashboards (we do not see individual visitor identities).

Information we do NOT collect

  • Payment card numbers or bank details (handled entirely by Etsy, Gumroad, or their payment processors).
  • Passwords or account credentials.
  • Data from cookies placed by our own website (we do not operate a direct e-commerce site with tracking cookies).

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Order fulfillment: To deliver your digital download or fulfil your physical order through our print-on-demand partner (Printify).
  • Customer support: To respond to questions, refund requests, or complaints.
  • Legal compliance: To comply with applicable laws, tax obligations, or marketplace requirements.
  • Business analytics: Aggregate, anonymized data to understand which products are popular and improve our offerings.

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for their marketing purposes.

4. Third Parties We Work With

To operate our shop, we share necessary order data with the following partners:

  • Etsy, Inc. — marketplace and payment processor. Etsy Privacy Policy
  • Gumroad, Inc. — digital product delivery platform. Gumroad Privacy Policy
  • Printify, Inc. — print-on-demand fulfilment (receives your shipping name and address for physical orders). Printify Privacy Policy
  • Anthropic, PBC — AI services used in our content creation workflow. Your personal data is not shared with Anthropic. Anthropic Privacy Policy
  • fal.ai — AI image generation used in our product creation workflow. Your personal data is not shared with fal.ai.

5. Social Media

We maintain accounts on Instagram, Facebook, Pinterest, and TikTok to promote our products. If you interact with our posts or follow our accounts, those platforms collect data according to their own privacy policies. We receive only aggregate engagement statistics (likes, saves, reach) — not your personal identity.

We do not run targeted advertising campaigns that use your personal data for ad targeting.

6. Data Retention

We retain order records for up to 7 years as required for tax and accounting purposes. Customer support correspondence is retained for 2 years. You may request deletion of your data (see Section 8) subject to legal retention requirements.

7. International Transfers

NovaCache operates from the United States. If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), United Kingdom, or another jurisdiction with data transfer restrictions, your data may be transferred to and processed in the United States. We rely on the data transfer mechanisms provided by our marketplace platforms (Etsy, Gumroad) which maintain appropriate safeguards.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

All customers

  • Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Request deletion of your data (subject to legal retention obligations).
  • Opt out of any direct marketing communications from us.

EEA / UK residents (GDPR)

  • Right to data portability.
  • Right to object to processing based on legitimate interests.
  • Right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.
  • Our lawful basis for processing is contract performance (fulfilling your order) and legitimate interests (operating our business).

California residents (CCPA / CPRA)

  • Right to know what personal information is collected and how it is used.
  • Right to delete personal information.
  • Right to opt out of the sale or sharing of personal information. We do not sell or share personal information.
  • Right to non-discrimination for exercising your privacy rights.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at christopher.d.raymond@gmail.com. We will respond within 30 days.

9. Children's Privacy

Our products and services are not directed at children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

10. Security

We take reasonable precautions to protect the data we hold, including limiting access and using secure communications. However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure. Payment data is never stored by us — it is handled entirely by our marketplace payment processors.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision. Continued use of our products after a change constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
