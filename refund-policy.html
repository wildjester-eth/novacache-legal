Refund & Return Policy

Last updated: January 1, 2025

We stand behind our products. This policy explains what happens if something goes wrong with your order.

Digital Downloads

Standard digital products No refund after download

Because digital files are delivered instantly and cannot be "returned," we generally do not offer refunds once a file has been successfully downloaded. This applies to:

  • Art prints (PNG, JPG, PDF)
  • Sticker sheets
  • Planner pages and journal templates
  • Greeting cards
  • Any other downloadable file

Exceptions — when we will refund We make it right

We will issue a full refund or replacement file if:

  • File is corrupted or undownloadable — the file you received is damaged and cannot be opened.
  • Wrong file delivered — you received a different product than what you purchased.
  • Significant technical defect — the file has a printing defect that was not visible in the listing preview (e.g., severe banding, missing portions of the design).

To request a refund or replacement under these exceptions, contact us at christopher.d.raymond@gmail.com within 14 days of purchase, with a description of the issue and a screenshot if applicable.

Please note: Colour differences between your screen and a printed output are normal and are not grounds for a refund. We recommend calibrating your printer settings and using the file's full resolution. Local print shops (FedEx Office, Staples, etc.) generally produce excellent results.

Print-on-Demand Physical Products

No returns for change of mind Final sale

Because each physical product (mug, notebook, tote bag, poster) is manufactured to order specifically for you, we cannot accept returns or exchanges for:

  • Change of mind or buyer's remorse
  • Ordering the wrong size (please check size guides before purchasing)
  • Slight colour variation from the product mockup image

Defective or damaged items Free replacement

We will send a free replacement or issue a full refund if your order arrives:

  • Damaged in transit (broken mug, crushed packaging)
  • With a significant printing defect (faded design, misaligned print, cracked coating)
  • Materially different from the product listing (wrong item shipped)

To claim a replacement:

  1. Email christopher.d.raymond@gmail.com within 30 days of the delivery date.
  2. Include your order number and 1–2 clear photos of the defect.
  3. We will arrange a replacement shipment or issue a refund within 5 business days.

Do not return the defective item — our print provider handles defect claims directly and a return is not required.

Lost or Missing Packages

If tracking shows your order as delivered but you did not receive it, please first check with neighbours and your building's mailroom. If the package is genuinely missing:

  • Contact us within 30 days of the estimated delivery date.
  • We will file a claim with the carrier and arrange a replacement if the carrier confirms the loss.
  • Packages marked as delivered to the correct address and lost after delivery are the buyer's responsibility — please ensure your shipping address is accurate at checkout.

Customs and Import Duties

International orders may be subject to customs duties or import taxes imposed by your country. These fees are the buyer's responsibility and are not included in the product price. We do not have control over customs processing times, which may extend delivery estimates.

How to Contact Us

For any refund, replacement, or order issue:

  • Email: christopher.d.raymond@gmail.com
  • Or use the "Message Seller" feature on Etsy

We aim to respond to all enquiries within 2 business days.
