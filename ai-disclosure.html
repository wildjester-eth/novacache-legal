AI & Copyright Disclosure

Last updated: January 1, 2025

In the interest of full transparency, NovaCache discloses the use of artificial intelligence tools in the creation of its products. This page explains how AI is used, what human oversight is applied, and what this means for you as a buyer.

1. AI-Assisted Design Creation

All NovaCache designs are created with the assistance of AI image generation technology, specifically Flux.1 Dev (provided by fal.ai). This technology uses machine learning to generate original artwork based on descriptive prompts written and reviewed by a human operator.

Every design goes through the following human-reviewed process before being listed for sale:

  1. Concept development — A human operator identifies a market niche and writes a creative prompt describing the desired artwork.
  2. Image generation — Flux.1 Dev generates one or more candidate images.
  3. Automated quality review — Each image is reviewed by an AI vision system (Claude, by Anthropic) to check for hallucinated text, distortions, or commercial quality issues.
  4. Human approval — The final product is reviewed and approved before listing.
  5. Listing creation — Product descriptions, titles, and tags are written with AI assistance and reviewed for accuracy and compliance.

2. Compliance with Marketplace Requirements

Etsy

Etsy requires sellers to disclose when a listing was created with AI-generative tools. All NovaCache listings on Etsy include the required AI disclosure and use the "AI-generated" tag where applicable. We comply with Etsy's Artificial Intelligence Policies.

Print-on-demand (Printify)

Physical NovaCache products are manufactured by Printify's network of print providers. Printify is a print-on-demand service — products are made individually when ordered. Our listings comply with Etsy's policy on print-on-demand and production partners, and all applicable disclosures are included in product listings.

3. Copyright and Originality

NovaCache takes intellectual property seriously. Our approach to copyright:

  • Original prompts: Every design begins with original creative direction written by a human. We do not copy or reproduce existing artwork.
  • No recognisable IP: We do not generate designs incorporating trademarked characters, logos, celebrities, copyrighted artworks, or other recognisable intellectual property belonging to third parties.
  • Ownership: The creative direction, curation, and commercial presentation of each design is the original work of NovaCache. NovaCache asserts ownership of the final product listings and associated trade dress.
  • Evolving law: AI-generated content and copyright is an area of evolving law. We monitor legal developments and will update our practices accordingly.

4. What You Can and Cannot Do with Our Designs

Personal use license (all purchases)

When you purchase a NovaCache product, you receive a personal, non-exclusive license to:

  • Print and display the design for personal, home, or office use.
  • Give the printed item as a personal gift.

Not permitted without a commercial license

  • Reproducing the design on products you sell commercially.
  • Redistributing or reselling the digital file itself.
  • Using the design in advertising or promotional materials for a business.
  • Claiming the design as your own original creation.
